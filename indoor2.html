Mermaid Beach Resort

The place has a lot of charm and creative art, the staff are all very friendly and helpful, and the management takes environmental and social responsibility very seriously. It's also a popular place for Bangladeshi visitors and foreigners, so you'll be sure to meet some fun people.

Bangabandhu Memorial Museum

This is the home of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib-ur Rahman, the Father of the Bangladeshi nation, who was instrumental in making Bangladesh an independent country. This is also the place where he was assasinated, the blood marks of which are now sealed below glass. The rooms have recently been renovated, but the sense of a nation trying to come to grips with its bloody history is evident.

Cloud Valley, Sajek

In 2016 Sajek valley become the most popular tourist destination in Bangladesh. A few years ago it was so difficult to reach this place because of the transportation service and some security problem. But nowadays anyone can go to this place directly by reserving Jeep or CNG or Bike. If you like hill, Nature, Sky you must to there. You become splashed by seeing its natural beauty specially when you will see that the cloud is touching your body then you will feel that you are living in the paradise.
